Testimonials

  • One Story and Aaron Weiss are the shepherds of our story at IT’S TIME TEXAS. We, therefore, hold storytelling, especially through the medium of video, in the highest regard.

    While storytelling is one of the oldest human traditions, it takes a special talent to be able to collect often diverse and distinct stories and weave together a coherent and compelling narrative that remains true to all individual contributors. We are so fortunate to have found that rare talent in Aaron and One Story. With One Story, we know that IT’S TIME TEXAS’ story is in very good hands.

    CEO, It’s Time Texas (Formerly ACTIVE Life)

There is question that we would be working with OSP again.
