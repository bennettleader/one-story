About

One Story Productions works with anyone who is doing good in the community and has a story to be told. Non-profits, foundations and companies have a need to show how their work plays a vital role in the community.

Each project is unique, so we work with clients to get an understanding of what’s important and how a brand film can best fit within larger marketing / brand strategy. Through collaboration, we create visual voices that tell real stories. We give a name and face to your mission–one that will attract an audience.

Production services include everything from the a one-man crew to a 10-person production. Post-production is where the story really takes shape...We work with clients until they love the final products.

About Aaron

About Aaron

Aaron Weiss
Founder, Principal

Aaron is the founder/principal at One Story Productions. He has helped over a hundred organizations tell their authentic story through the production of films. His passion for cause related films has taken him around the world to reveal stories that need to be experienced.

In 2007, Aaron realized that nonprofit organizations stand up for people who don’t have a voice. The organizations create awareness, innovation and change and have an empowering story that deserves to be told.

Aaron engages with the community through speaking roles and as a freelance director working with agencies. Through consulting, he shows organizations the value of telling stories through this medium.

Aaron actively seeks ways to engage with community through board leadership and volunteerism.
