About

One Story Productions partners with non-profits and businesses to tell the story behind their missions.

Recognizing that non-profits, foundations and companies have a need to demonstrate their work’s vital role in the community, One Story Productions launched in 2007. For each client we create a unique brand development plan. First we sit down together. We document goals and expectations based on an understanding of what’s important and how a brand film can best fit within larger marketing / brand strategy.

Through collaboration, we create visual voices that tell real stories. Production services can span from a one-man crew to a 10-person production. Post-production is detailed and unique—this is where the story takes shape, so we work with clients until they love the final products.

About Aaron

About Aaron

Aaron Weiss
Founder, Principal

Aaron is the founder and principal at One Story Productions. He has worked with over 100 organizations to tell their authentic story through the production of films. His passion for cause-related films has taken him around the world to reveal stories that need to be experienced.

In 2007 on a trip to capture the story of an Arts Conservatory in India, Aaron recognized that nonprofit organizations stand up for people who don’t have a voice. It’s his belief that these organizations create awareness, innovation and change and have an empowering story that deserves to be told. Documentary filmmaking plays a very similar role as nonprofits as these nonprofits and change is possible.

Aaron actively builds the One Story Production’s community involvement through speaking roles and as a freelance director. Aaron takes on many roles, working with agencies and consulting. As an expert in the field, he shows organizations the value of telling stories through this medium.

Contact Aaron for speaking and consulting opportunities.
