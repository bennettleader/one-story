We have worked with ACTIVE Life and IT’S TIME TEXAS since 2010. ACTIVE Life is a social change nonprofit working to organize and accelerate the movement to make healthy the norm.

Background

We’ve travelled around the state to produce projects that help them unite and mobilize individuals, institutions, and organizations to build healthier communities.

IT’S TIME TEXAS (ITT) is a first-of-its-kind cause that motivates and mobilizes people to make healthy the norm where they live, learn, work, worship, and shop.
